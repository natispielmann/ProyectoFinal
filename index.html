Carolina Spielmann

I'm a German-Argentinean artist and illustrator living between Paris and London. After graduating from the prestigious Parsons School of Design, I have exhibited my artwork in galleries and fairs around the world, published several illustration books and collaborated with editorial companies as well as advertising agencies (Florence Biennale of Contemporary Art, M6, Lowe, Rosa Park).

I have developed artist projects with renown brands (ClubMed, Campari, Heineken, Habitat, Converse) and am currently a post-graduate student at Central Saint Martins in London while pursuing my career in the creative industries and the art world.
